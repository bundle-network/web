So, you would like to trade crypto currencies?

Just as the number of coins and tokens has increased exponentially to well over a thousand, the number and variety of exchange platforms has skyrocketed to over 200. That’s why only 10% of the coins traded on the market are available even on the largest, most well-known platforms. .

 

Availability

The market already has more than +2,000 coins/tokens and +200 exchanges. This fragmentation makes it impossible to find every token at every exchange platform.

Liquidity

Due to the availability problem, tokens get very limited demand and supply. Which leads to very low figures of daily trade.

Arbitrage

Due to liquidity problem, we see huge price differences between exchanges. Even for the same token, there are huge arbitrage differences which leads to an inefficient market.

Price Fluctuations

Due to low trading volume, prices can go up / down 20 % - 30 % in a day. Which creates an image of immature market for newcomers.

What is Bundle Network?

Bundle Network is an online platform connected to various exchanges, enabling users to purchase and sell multiple coins & tokens within seconds of execution through one account and one click, at the best price possible either in bundles, or individually. It will also serve as an online meeting point for cryptocurrency investors to share and discuss topics related to the trading cryptocurrencies.

What is bundle token?

Bundle Token will be the utility token at our platform. It will not represent the shares of Bundle Network LTD.

What are the functionalities of the token?
  1. Lower Commission Rates: Users performing transactions in the Bundle Platform pay lower fees.
    • The fee is 0.2% for standard transaction paid with Bitcoin.
    • The fee is 0.1% for standard transaction paid with the Bundle Token (BND).
  2. BNDL Token holders will have priority for some services.
  3. BNDL Token holders will have rights to vote on new features that will be implemented on our platform.
What is the core revenue model?
  • Our core revenue model is commission based. We charge an industry average trading fee of 0.2%. If the fee is paid by BNDL Token, there will be a 50% discount and the fee is going to be 0.1%. Since we are going to be providing high trading volume to the exchanges, the fee we pay will be less than usual. The difference between our users pay and we pay to the exchanges is going to be Bundle Network’s profit.
How does social trading work?

At Bundle Network there are two types of traders; regular traders and trade experts. Anyone can copy a trade expert’s portfolio or trade. This will give a jumpstart for less experienced traders and experienced traders will have a chance to monetize their experiences. Also everyone in the Bundle Network platform will be able to share their trade ideas with each other.
