Current Market Problems

Availability

The market already has more than +1,000 coins/tokens and +100 exchanges

This fragmentation makes it impossible to find every token at every exchange platform

Liquidity

Due to the availability problem, tokens get very limited demand and supply

Which leads to very low figures of daily trade

Arbitrage

Due to liquidity problem, we see huge price differences between exchanges

Even for the same token, there are huge arbitrage differences which leads to an inefficient market

Price Fluctuations

Due to low trading volume, prices can go up / down 20 % - 30 % in a day

Which creates an image of immature market for newcomers

Solutions of Bundle Network

Access ‘em all

Bundle network will be connected to at least 10+ different exchanges to enable a wider access to +90% of the market from one single point

This access will pile the tokens and exchanges up, which are already very fragmentated

Welcome the newcomers

Bundle Network aims to welcome the masses into the crypto trading world thanks to its simple trading interface and unique gateway feature

This will eliminate the complications of current market place; the market will have more contributors which will lead to bigger liquidity

Optimize the market

Bundle Network will display real time prices at +10 different exchanges and enable instant trading of these coins/tokens at those prices

This will eventually diminish the arbitrage differences between the exchanges and help the market to perform more efficiently

Higher volume, less fluctuation

By the increased volume of contributors, coins/tokens will meet a wider audience

Wider audience will lead to wider liquidity and less fluctuating prices then before

FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions
What is bundle network?
Bundle Network is an online platform connected to various exchanges, enabling users to purchase and sell multiple coins & tokens within seconds of execution through one account and one click, at the best price possible either in bundles, or individually. It will also serve as an online meeting point for cryptocurrency investors to share and discuss topics related to the trading cryptocurrencies.
What is bundle token?
Bundle Token will be used at our platform for two different functionalities. It will neither represent the shares of Bundle Network LLC nor it gives any kind of voting rights for the holders.
What are the functionalities of the token?
  1. Lower Commission Rates: Users performing transactions in the Bundle Platform pay lower fees.
    • The fee is 0.5% for standard transaction paid with Bitcoin or Ethereum .
    • The fee is 0.2% for standard transaction paid with the Bundle Token (BND).
  2. Determine Transaction Speed & Frequency:
    • Basic User:
      • No minimum amount deposited
      • 10 transactions per day
      • 5 min waiting time between transactions
    • Pro User:
      • 300 BND deposit
      • 30 transactions per day
      • 0 waiting time between transactions
    • Master User:
      • 1000 BND deposit
      • Unlimited number of transactions
      • 0 waiting time between transactions
  3. Insurance
    • Since today's tech world is ever evolving, we introduce another first of its kind service, providing insurance to the users’ funds on the Bundle Network.
    • The Bundle Network will select exchange partners who qualify above a certain security score. In addition, distribution of funds across different exchanges decreases risk.
    • In return for the annual premiums paid with Bundle Tokens, we insure funds up to 1,500,000 BND tokens (USD 150K according to TGE prices). Here are the three levels of insurance premium options and the amounts they cover

    BND Insurance Premium Coverage Limits
    Advantage Pack (BND) 1,000 (multiplier) X 5 50,000
    Safe Pack (BND) 10,000 (multiplier) X 8 400,000
    Full Safe Pack (BND) 50,000 (multiplier) X 10 1,500,000
What is the core revenue model?
  • The daily trading volume for cryptocurrencies is more than 6 billion dollars per day
  • Bundle Network aims to have 1% market share at 2018 as the normal case scenerio
  • Which will yield a USD 60M of daily trading volume
  • Which will yield a USD 300K revenue per day

How does the network function work?
A Bundle Manager (who can use the platform in order to create their own bundles) may share their successful Bundles with others, on the platform, thereby creating a communication channel for the cryptocurrency ecosystem. They will be ranked by their successful trading and receive commissions via Bundle Tokens based on the usage of their bundles. As a result, the newcomers would see the best performers and benefit from their knowledge and experience while they are making their future purchases
